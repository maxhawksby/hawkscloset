The Person
Behind It

I'm Maxwell. Grew up in New Jersey with an eye for things other people walked past. Started Hawks Closet because I believed every piece has a story — and a price. I source obsessively: thrift runs, estate sales, yard sales at 6am, trunks full of stuff most people don't know what to do with. Everything that ends up here has been through my hands and earned its place. No filler. No bulk. Just pieces that matter, sold by someone who understands why they do. The fit has to be right. The price has to be right. Everything has a price.
